# How to connect to Regal Support

### Real Time in-App Issues

Use the Chat icon at the bottom left of your screen when you are logged into your account at <https://app.regalvoice.com/agent>.

We will respond to you as quickly as possible.

Alternatively, you can email us at support@regalvoice.com.

### Bugs and Feature Requests

Submit an official ticket [here](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/requests/new). Please provide as much detail and context as possible in the ticket. Screenshots, device and browser information are also helpful.
