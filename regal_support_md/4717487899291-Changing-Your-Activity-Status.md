# Changing Your Activity Status

### You'll need to set your availability before you're able to start viewing tasks, making calls and messaging customers.

Once you are logged in and viewing your [**Agent Desktop**](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/4689702368667-Agent-Guide-The-Agent-Desktop), click the headset icon in the upper right hand corner of the Agent Desktop and set yourself to **Available**. When you are in the Available status, you are eligible to start receiving tasks.

#### Use the Availability Selector to set your status.

![status_dropdown.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/27643827632027)

By default, Regal only provides two statuses: **Available** and **Unavailable.**However, more options can be added according to your needs. Your managers can add, edit, delete, and reorder statuses such as "Break" or "Training" for example, in [Settings](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/45677618896283).

Ultimately, despite whatever statuses are available in your Regal instance, setting yourself to a status with a green indicator dot means you are able to handle tasks. While statuses with a gray indicator dot will make you unavailable and ineligible to receive tasks.

> ### 🚧  Remember to go offline when you're away!
>
> At the end of the day, or if you step away from your desk for a break during the day, make sure to set yourself to an unavailable status so that you're not being assigned tasks when you aren't able to accept them!

Regal will automatically flip agents to the **Unavailable** status when they log out of the app. If Regal detects an agent in an **Available** status has been inactive for 30 minutes, that agent will also be flipped to the **Unavailable** status. Browser inactivity occurs when an agent exits the browser, closes their computer, or loses internet connection. Agents can also choose to flip to a custom **Unavailable** status and stay there indefinitely, such as "Out Of Office". Because this is an **Unavailable** status, Regal will not force them into the default **Unavailable** status.
