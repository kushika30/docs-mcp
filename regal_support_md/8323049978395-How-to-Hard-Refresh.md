# How to Hard Refresh

A **hard refresh** is a common troubleshooting step that clears your browser cache for a specific page, which forces it to load the most recent version of that page.

**Note**: *Clicking the "Refresh" arrow on your browser address bar is **not**a hard refresh. Here's how you can clear your cache through a hard refresh on in Chrome:*

1. **Logout of Regal.io**
2. **Right-click anywhere on the login screen**
3. **Select “Inspect” in the pop-up window**![mceclip0.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/8322882221723)
4. **Code window will appear**![mceclip1.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/8322888144667)
5. **Right click refresh button**![mceclip3.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/8322956723483)
6. **Click “Empty cache and hard reload”***![mceclip4.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/8323009676827)*
7. **Log Back into Regal**
