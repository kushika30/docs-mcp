# Add a New Phone Number to Your Regal Account

Regal is able to purchase and add new phone numbers to your Regal Contact Center by request. Both local and toll-free numbers are available. Please consult your Account Manger for pricing and/or reach out to [support@regal.ai](mailto:support@regal.ai) to purchase numbers for your account.

Once purchased, your number(s) will appear in your Regal account and you can add and configure them for [IVRs](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/23151204496283-How-to-Configure-IVR-in-Regal), [Campaigns](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/5721270757275-What-are-Phone-Call-Campaigns), and/or [Spam/Branding](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/11629901426331-How-to-Add-Branded-Caller-ID-and-Remove-Carrier-Spam-Filtering-on-Outbound-Calls) based on your use case.
