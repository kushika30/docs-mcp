# Logging in to Your Regal Account

### There are 3 ways to log in to Regal. Ask your manager, if you are unsure of which method you should use:

**1. Login with your email and password** - Visit app.regalvoice.com.

#### Enter your email

![Screen_Shot_2022-03-14_at_2.33.34_PM.png](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/4689216203803/Screen_Shot_2022-03-14_at_2.33.34_PM.png)

#### Then enter your password

![](https://files.readme.io/36030ef-Password_filled.png "Click to close...")

**2. Sign in with Google** - Visit app.regalvoice.com. Enter your email, and if your company has elected to require Login with Google, you will be presented with a **Sign In with Google** button. Click that button, and when prompted, select your **work** email account and login.

#### Sign in with Google

![Sign in with Google](https://files.readme.io/f0ab9d8-logingoogle.png "logingoogle.png")

**3. Login with Okta** - If your company has set up Login with Okta, you will not be able to log in directly from app.regalvoice.com. Instead, **you must visit your company's Okta instance**, and select the Regal app from there.

#### Login with Okta

![Login with Okta](https://files.readme.io/ccd3f5c-Group_873.png "Group 873.png")
