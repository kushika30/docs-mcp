# How To Set Up My Device to Use Regal

### In this quick tutorial, we'll walk you through the preliminary steps you should take before using Regal.

#### Network Environment

Regal is only accessible through your web browser with a stable Internet connection. Please note that Regal is currently *only* supported on Desktop & Laptop devices - Mobile devices may experience limited functionality and/or errors. For security purposes you may need to allow [Regal](https://app.regalvoice.com/login) to access various devices and applications on your computer when prompted.

To start, we recommend that you use a wired Ethernet connection. This will ensure minimum interruption to your Regal experience. We suggest a minimum download speed of 25 Mbps when placing or accepting calls in Regal. Once you are wired to your modem, be sure to turn off your WiFi.

- On MacOS select the WiFi icon in your control menu on the top of your screen and toggle it off.
- On WindowsOS, right click the WiFi icon in the bottom right of your screen and disable it.

#### 

#### Browser

Use Google Chrome when you are logging into Regal. This is our only supported browser. You can download Google Chrome [here](https://www.google.com/chrome/?brand=YTUH&geo=US&gclid=CjwKCAiA9tyQBhAIEiwA6tdCrDIc2QQq2SRLaZjU6sWoIzPDT3p8INTYCGjb-vXHNbpLqIySVJQydRoCDkoQAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds). Regal tests our UI against the latest version of Chrome weekly - we recommend updating to new Chrome releases two weeks after their original release date.

#### 

#### Microphone

If you will be placing and taking calls using Regal, we recommend using a dedicated Analog headset for the best quality in your phone conversations. The analog headset plugs into the 3.5mm headphone socket on a computer. A USB port, or digital audio port headset is an okay alternative. We do not recommend using bluetooth/wireless headsets when making or receiving calls with Regal

Next, you need to allow Regal to access your microphone and send notifications about new tasks.

- Select the three dots in the top right corner of your Chrome browser window.
- Select *Settings*, and on the left side of the settings page select *Privacy and* *Security*
- Select *Site Settings*, and then select "Notifications", add "[https://app.regal.io/"](https://app.regalvoice.com:443") to the list of "Allowed to send notifications"
- Select the "<-" at the top of the page to go back to the site settings page, then select "Microphone".

  - Ensure Regal is not listed under "Not allowed to use your microphone".
  - Ensure that "Sites can ask to use your microphone" is checked.
  - Once you login to Regal, place a call to yourself and click "Allow" when prompted to allow Regal to use your microphone. Ensure that there are [no call quality issues](https://support.regal.ai/hc/en-us/articles/4466469337755-Troubleshooting-Call-Issues) before you start accepting tasks from the queue.

Lastly, we recommend that you minimize activity across other websites and devices when using Regal.
