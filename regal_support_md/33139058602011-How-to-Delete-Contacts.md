# How to Delete Contacts

### **All admins** can delete contacts directly in-app, one at a time:

- Navigate to Audience tab to search for the contact
- Click the target contact to open the side panel
- Click "Delete Contact" at the bottom
- **Deletion cannot be easily reversed. Please ensure correct profile is selected.**

![](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/33139114201243)

- Confirm delete after reading messages

![](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/33139058590875)

### Other ways to delete contacts:

- Tasks > find the event associated with the target contact > delete contact

![](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/33240157426715)

- Dynamic Segments > search contact using contact attribute condition > delete contact

![](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/33139114206363)

- Recent Activity > find the event associated with the target contact > delete contact

![](https://support.regal.ai/hc/article_attachments/33139114209051)

### Expectations

1. All information related to the deleted contacts are removed from the app immediately. This includes contact profile, conversation history, and past events. The contact will no longer be searchable anywhere.
2. Contacts will also be removed from in-progress Journeys.
3. Contacts can be created again with the same phone / email in the future. But previous history will not be restored.
4. Outstanding tasks for the contact are not automatically deleted. **Prior to deleting the contact,** contact's outstanding tasks should be canceled. Navigate to Tasks and searching by the phone number.

If you have other questions about contact deletion, please contact support.
