# Gong.io

This guide describes how to send Regal recordings to Gong.io.

# Enable Gong Integration

**Step 1:** Enable Import calls for your agents in Gong. Go to Gong > Data capture settings > Telephony system calls > Import calls.

![](https://files.readme.io/6e1f8e1-Group_674.png)

**Step 2:** Get your Gong Access Key and Secret. Go to Gong > Workspace settings > API.

Click "GET API KEY" to generate and then copy the Access Key

![](https://files.readme.io/049c215-api.png)

Click "Show secret" to review and then copy the Secret

![](https://files.readme.io/86378a5-secret.png)

**Step 3:** Email your Gong Access Key and Secret to [[email protected].](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#97e4e2e7e7f8e5e3d7e5f2f0f6fbb9fef8b9) (You can use <https://pwpush.com> to share it in a more secure way)

> ## 📘
>
> Wait for Regal Confirmation
>
> Once we have your Gong Access Key and Secret, we will respond within a couple of hours and let you know that you're all set. You should then see recordings flowing to Gong on future calls.
