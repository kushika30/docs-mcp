# Increase Outbound Answer Rates

For outbound phone calls, Regal has a number of levers to increase your answer rates:

- **[Spam Remediation](https://www.regal.ai/spam-remediation)** - Avoid your outbound calls appearing as "Spam Likely" on your customer's cell phone
- **[Branded Caller ID & Call Logo Branding](https://www.regal.ai/branded-caller-id)** - Automatically display your brand name or logo on your customer's cell phone when they receive your call
- **Local Presence** - Automatically call from an area cost that matches the customer's area code
- **Dynamic Phone Number Inventory Management** (leveraging our [outboundANI integration](/docs/outboundani)) - automatically dial from the optimal phone number for each dial based on real-time analytics

*Note: for the last 2 options, you'd need to use Regal as the dialer.*
