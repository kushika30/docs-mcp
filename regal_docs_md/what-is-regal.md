# What is Regal?

Power better inbound and outbound calls for support, sales and operations – with way less effort – using the Regal AI Agent Platform.

Regal gives enterprise companies the tools to transform customer communications with delightful AI Agents that sound like humans, are connected to your data, easy to customize and monitor, always-available, and ready to take action.

- Build once and deploy across all channels - voice, sms and chat
- Connect with your systems so your AI Agents can take action
- Route inbound, orchestrate outbound, and tailor each interaction based on customer data
- Blend your human agents with Regal’s AI Agents for optimal collaboration
- Leverage simulations and A/B testing for continuous improvement
- Get insights into what's working and what isn’t to achieve desired business results
