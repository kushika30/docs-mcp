# Abstrakt

[Abstrakt](https://abstrakt.ai/why-abstrakt/about/) allows you to create Automated playbooks for your agents to ensure they follow your best practices and coaching.

- With automated playbooks, always know the right talking points or compliance statements.
- With recommended responses, never miss an objection or handle a question incorrectly again.

Abstrakt playbooks work on top of the Regal Agent Desktop. The way the Abstrakt integration knows when to display each playbook for a new call is based on Regal's Reporting event webhooks.
